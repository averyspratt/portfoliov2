SQRRL

Sqrrl is a Cambridge-based startup specializing in cybersecurity analytics. Sqrrl provides complex data visualizations based on link analysis, machine learning algorithms, and multi-petabyte scalability capabilities to aid security analysts in efficient and effective threat hunting. I spent the summer of 2017 working directly with their head of Product Experience as Sqrrl's UX Intern.

User Experience, Data Visualization, Start-Up, Dashboard Design

+ Designed UX solutions for Sqrrl's KOLA release
+ Developed User Personas and Demo Stories
+ Worked with Product Team to design future directions for the product
+ Wireframe Design
