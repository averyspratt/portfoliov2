INDOOR
PLANTER

Inspiried by my senior capstone project, I decided I wanted to build an indoor planter. This planter, designed to hang in a window, would make a great addition to any room and makes growing house plants and herbs indoors easy. But wait! It doesnt stop there. Promoting accessibility through design is one of my passions. That's why I made an instruction booklet so you could make your own window planter at home! Because everyone deserves a little green in their life.

Woodworking, Product Design, Illustration, Graphic Design

THE PLANTER

To build this planter you will need particle board, a circular or hand saw, a jigsaw, hammer and nails, woodglue, a screw and wide drillbit, sandpaper, primer, paint, hooks, and a plant light
